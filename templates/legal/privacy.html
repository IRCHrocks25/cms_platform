Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

When you use sites.katek.app — either directly or embedded inside a connected GoHighLevel sub-account — we collect:

  • Account details you provide (name, email, the site content you create).
  • Authentication metadata (sessions, login timestamps).
  • Operational logs (request paths, error traces) used to keep the service running.
  • If installed as a GHL app: the location ID and basic user identity passed to us by GHL so we can route you to the right site.

What we do with it

We use this data only to operate the service, authenticate you, and serve the sites you publish. We do not sell personal data and we do not use it for advertising.

Where it lives

Data is stored on infrastructure we operate in the EU and US. Access is restricted to the operators of this service.

Your choices

You can request export or deletion of your data at any time by contacting your agency administrator.

Contact

Questions? Email your agency administrator or reach out to the operators of this site.
